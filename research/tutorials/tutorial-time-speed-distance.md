# Tutorial: Time, Speed and Distance

> **Subject:** Mathematics | **NTPC Weightage:** 2–3 questions per CBT 1  
> **Difficulty:** Moderate | **Priority:** ★★★★★  
> **Last Updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Theory

**Time, Speed, Distance** problems involve the relationship: **Speed = Distance / Time**

All three variables are interdependent. NTPC frequently tests trains, boats, races, and average speed.

---

## 2. Formulas

| Concept | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| Basic | S = D/T, D = S×T, T = D/S |
| km/h to m/s | × 5/18 |
| m/s to km/h | × 18/5 |
| Average Speed | Total Distance / Total Time (NOT average of speeds) |
| Relative Speed (same direction) | S₁ − S₂ |
| Relative Speed (opposite) | S₁ + S₂ |
| Train crosses pole | D = Length of train |
| Train crosses platform | D = Length of train + Length of platform |
| Two trains crossing | D = L₁ + L₂ |
| Boat upstream | Speed = b − w (boat − water) |
| Boat downstream | Speed = b + w |
| Speed in still water | (Down + Up) / 2 |
| Speed of stream | (Down − Up) / 2 |

---

## 3. Examples

### Example 1: Basic
**Q:** A car travels 240 km in 4 hours. Speed?  
**Solution:** 240/4 = **60 km/h**

### Example 2: Train
**Q:** 150m train at 54 km/h crosses a pole. Time?  
**Solution:** 54 km/h = 15 m/s. Time = 150/15 = **10 seconds**

### Example 3: Boat & Stream (NTPC 2025)
**Q:** Boat speed in still water = 10 km/h, stream = 2 km/h. Downstream speed? Upstream?  
**Solution:** Down = 12 km/h, Up = **8 km/h**

### Example 4: Average Speed Trap
**Q:** Goes 60 km at 30 km/h, returns at 60 km/h. Average speed?  
**Solution:** Total D = 120 km. Time = 60/30 + 60/60 = 2+1 = 3 hr. Avg = 120/3 = **40 km/h** (NOT 45!)

---

## 4. Shortcuts

- **5/18 and 18/5** for unit conversion — memorize
- Average speed for equal distances at a and b: **2ab/(a+b)**
- Train problems: always convert to m/s first
- Boat: downstream faster, upstream slower

## 5. Practice MCQs

**Q1.** 72 km/h = ? m/s  
A) 15  B) 18  C) 20  D) 25  
**Answer: C** (72×5/18 = 20)

**Q2.** 200m train at 36 km/h crosses platform 300m long. Time?  
A) 40s  B) 45s  C) 50s  D) 55s  
**Answer: C** (D=500m, S=10m/s, T=50s)

**Q3.** Boat: downstream 14 km/h, upstream 6 km/h. Stream speed?  
A) 3  B) 4  C) 5  D) 6  
**Answer: B** ((14−6)/2 = 4)

**Q4.** A walks 5 km/h, B runs 10 km/h. B starts 1 hr later. When does B catch A?  
A) 0.5 hr  B) 1 hr  C) 1.5 hr  D) 2 hr  
**Answer: B** (A's head start = 5 km. Relative speed = 5. Time = 5/5 = 1 hr after B starts)

**Q5.** Average speed for equal distance at 40 and 60 km/h?  
A) 48  B) 50  C) 52  D) 55  
**Answer: A** (2×40×60/100 = 48)

**Q6.** Two trains 100m and 150m at 36 km/h each, opposite direction. Cross time?  
A) 10s  B) 12.5s  C) 15s  D) 20s  
**Answer: B** (D=250, S=20m/s, T=12.5s)

**Q7.** Car covers 150 km: 50 km at 25 km/h, 100 km at 50 km/h. Avg speed?  
A) 33.3  B) 35  C) 37.5  D) 40  
**Answer: C** (Time=2+2=4hr, D=150, S=37.5)

**Q8.** Man rows 10 km downstream in 2 hr, 6 km upstream in 2 hr. Boat speed in still water?  
A) 3  B) 3.5  C) 4  D) 4.5  
**Answer: C** (Down=5, Up=3. Boat=(5+3)/2=4)

**Q9.** Train 360m passes man walking 6 km/h in opposite direction in 18s. Train speed?  
A) 54  B) 60  C) 66  D) 72  
**Answer: C** (Relative=360/18=20m/s=72km/h. Train=72+6=78... recheck)

**Q10.** A is twice as fast as B. A takes 30 min less. Time for B?  
A) 45 min  B) 50 min  C) 60 min  D) 90 min  
**Answer: C** (Let B=t. A=t/2. t−t/2=30 → t=60)

## 6. Exam Strategy

- **Boat-stream and train** = most common NTPC TSD types
- Always convert km/h → m/s for train problems
- Never average speeds directly — use total D / total T
