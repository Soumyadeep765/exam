# Tutorial: General Science for NTPC

> **Subject:** General Awareness | **NTPC Weightage:** 10–12 questions per CBT 1  
> **Difficulty:** Moderate | **Priority:** ★★★★★  
> **Last Updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Theory

General Science is the **single highest-weightage GA topic** — 25–30% of all GA questions. Based on **NCERT Class 6–10** science. RRB tests application-based questions, not just facts.

---

## 2. Physics — Key Topics

| Topic | Key Facts | NTPC Frequency |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| Motion | Speed, velocity, acceleration. Newton's 3 laws | High |
| Force & Pressure | F=ma, Pascal's law, atmospheric pressure | High |
| Work & Energy | W=Fs, KE=½mv², PE=mgh, conservation | Medium |
| Light | Reflection, refraction, mirror/lens formulas | High |
| Sound | Speed ~343 m/s in air, echo, frequency | Medium |
| Electricity | Ohm's law V=IR, series/parallel, power | High |
| Magnetism | Field lines, electromagnet, motor principle | Medium |
| Heat | Conduction, convection, radiation, expansion | High |

### Must-Know Formulas
- Speed = Distance/Time
- Force = mass × acceleration
- Work = Force × displacement
- Power = Work/Time
- Ohm's Law: V = IR
- Mirror: 1/f = 1/v + 1/u
- Lens: Same formula

## 5. Previous Year Questions (Pattern-Based)

**PYQ (NTPC 2025):** Acid, Bases and Salt — pH of pure water?  
**Answer:** 7 (neutral)

**PYQ (NTPC 2025):** Cell — powerhouse of the cell?  
**Answer:** Mitochondria

**PYQ (NTPC 2024):** Which gas is produced during photosynthesis?  
**Answer:** Oxygen

**PYQ (NTPC 2023):** Speed of light in vacuum?  
**Answer:** 3 × 10⁸ m/s

## 6. Practice MCQs

**Q1.** pH of pure water is:  
A) 0  B) 7  C) 14  D) 1  
**Answer: B**

**Q2.** Powerhouse of the cell:  
A) Nucleus  B) Ribosome  C) Mitochondria  D) Golgi body  
**Answer: C**

**Q3.** Ohm's law:  
A) V = I/R  B) V = IR  C) V = I+R  D) V = I-R  
**Answer: B**

**Q4.** Scurvy is caused by deficiency of:  
A) Vitamin A  B) Vitamin B  C) Vitamin C  D) Vitamin D  
**Answer: C**

**Q5.** Most reactive metal:  
A) Iron  B) Gold  C) Potassium  D) Copper  
**Answer: C**

**Q6.** Gas produced in photosynthesis:  
A) CO₂  B) O₂  C) N₂  D) H₂  
**Answer: B**

**Q7.** Speed of sound in air (approx):  
A) 343 m/s  B) 1500 m/s  C) 3000 m/s  D) 3×10⁸ m/s  
**Answer: A**

**Q8.** Universal donor blood group:  
A) A  B) B  C) AB  D) O  
**Answer: D**

**Q9.** Chemical formula of common salt:  
A) NaCl  B) KCl  C) CaCl₂  D) NaOH  
**Answer: A**

**Q10.** Newton's third law:  
A) F=ma  B) Action-reaction equal and opposite  C) Inertia  D) Gravity  
**Answer: B**

## 7. Study Strategy

1. **Read NCERT Science Class 6–10** (highest ROI)
2. Focus on **tables** (vitamins, metals, organs)
3. Practice **application questions** ("Why does X happen?")
4. Revise **Acid-Base-Salt** and **Cell/Human Body** thoroughly
5. Target: **8–10 correct out of 10–12** science questions
