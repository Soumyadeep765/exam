# Tutorial: Time and Work

> **Subject:** Mathematics | **NTPC Weightage:** 1–3 questions per CBT 1  
> **Difficulty:** Moderate–Tough | **Priority:** ★★★★  
> **Last Updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Theory

**Time and Work** problems deal with the rate at which individuals or groups complete tasks.

- If A completes work in **n days** → A's 1-day work = **1/n**
- Combined work: add individual rates
- **Efficiency ∝ 1/Time** (faster worker = more efficient)
- **MDH Formula:** M₁D₁H₁/W₁ = M₂D₂H₂/W₂ (Men, Days, Hours, Work)

---

## 2. Formulas

| Concept | Formula |
|---------|---------|
| Work done in 1 day | 1/Time |
| Total work | LCM of individual times (convenient) |
| Combined (A+B) | 1/A + 1/B per day |
| Three workers | 1/A + 1/B + 1/C |
| A is n times efficient as B | A's time = B's time / n |
| Work = Efficiency × Time | W = E × T |
| Wages ∝ Work done | Share = Individual work / Total work |
| Alternate days | Calculate day-by-day or use combined rate |
| Pipe & Cistern (fill) | Same as work (positive rate) |
| Pipe & Cistern (empty) | Negative rate (outlet) |

### Pipe & Cistern
- Inlet pipe: fills → rate = +1/t
- Outlet pipe: empties → rate = −1/t
- Combined: 1/A − 1/B (if B is outlet)

## 3. Examples

### Example 1: Basic Combined Work
**Q:** A completes work in 10 days, B in 15 days. Together?  
**Solution:**  
1/10 + 1/15 = 3/30 + 2/30 = 5/30 = 1/6  
**Together: 6 days**

### Example 2: Efficiency
**Q:** A is twice as efficient as B. A takes 12 days. B takes?  
**Solution:** B takes 12 × 2 = **24 days**

### Example 3: Wages Distribution
**Q:** A and B complete work in 6 and 8 days respectively. They work together and earn ₹4200. A's share?  
**Solution:**  
Work ratio = 1/6 : 1/8 = 4:3  
A's share = (4/7) × 4200 = **₹2400**

### Example 4: Pipe & Cistern
**Q:** Pipe A fills tank in 12 hrs, B in 8 hrs. Together?  
**Solution:** 1/12 + 1/8 = 5/24 → **24/5 = 4.8 hours**

### Example 5: Alternate Days (NTPC Pattern)
**Q:** A alone: 10 days. B alone: 15 days. They work alternately starting with A. Total time?  
**Solution:**  
In 2 days: 1/10 + 1/15 = 5/30 = 1/6  
After 6 cycles (12 days): 6/6 = 1 (complete)  
Check: 6 cycles × 2 days = **12 days**

## 4. Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Application |
|----------|-------------|
| LCM method | Set total work = LCM of times |
| Efficiency ratio | If times 6:8, efficiency 4:3 |
| Quick combined | AB together = AB/(A+B) days |
| MDH formula | For varying men/days/hours |
| Outlet pipe | Subtract rate instead of add |

## 5. Previous Year Questions (Pattern-Based)

**PYQ (NTPC 2021):** A can do work in 20 days, B in 30 days. A works for 5 days alone, then B joins. Remaining days?  
**Solution:** A does 5/20 = 1/4. Remaining 3/4. Combined rate = 1/20+1/30 = 5/60 = 1/12. Days = (3/4)/(1/12) = 9. Total = 5+9 = 14 days.

**PYQ (NTPC 2025):** Pipe A fills in 6 hrs, B empties in 8 hrs. Tank is full, both opened. Time to empty?  
**Solution:** Net = −1/8 + 1/6 = (−3+4)/24 = 1/24... Wait, if tank is full and both open: fill rate 1/6, empty rate 1/8. Net = 1/6 − 1/8 = 4/24 − 3/24 = 1/24. Actually fills in 24 hrs. Re-read: if A fills 6hr, B empties 8hr, both open on empty tank → fills in 24 hrs.

## 6. Practice MCQs

**Q1.** A: 12 days, B: 18 days. Together = ?  
A) 6.2 days  B) 7.2 days  C) 8 days  D) 9 days  
**Answer: B** (1/12+1/18 = 5/36 → 36/5 = 7.2)

**Q2.** A is 3 times as efficient as B. B takes 24 days. A takes?  
A) 6 days  B) 8 days  C) 12 days  D) 16 days  
**Answer: B** (24/3 = 8)

**Q3.** A+B: 6 days. B+C: 8 days. A+C: 7.2 days. A alone?  
A) 10 days  B) 12 days  C) 15 days  D) 18 days  
**Answer: B** (2(A+B+C) = 1/6+1/8+1/36 = ... solve: A = 12 days)

**Q4.** 12 men complete work in 9 days. 18 men in?  
A) 4 days  B) 5 days  C) 6 days  D) 7 days  
**Answer: C** (12×9 = 18×D → D = 6)

**Q5.** A fills in 10 hrs, B empties in 15 hrs. Both open on empty tank. Time to fill?  
A) 20 hrs  B) 25 hrs  C) 30 hrs  D) 35 hrs  
**Answer: C** (1/10 − 1/15 = 1/30 → 30 hrs)

**Q6.** A: 8 days, B: 12 days. Worked 2 days together, then A left. B finishes in?  
A) 5 days  B) 6 days  C) 7 days  D) 8 days  
**Answer: C** (Done = 2×(1/8+1/12) = 2×5/24 = 5/12. Left = 7/12. B: (7/12)/(1/12) = 7)

**Q7.** 15 men, 8 days → 20 men, ? days  
A) 5  B) 6  C) 7  D) 8  
**Answer: B**

**Q8.** A+B = 12 days. B = 20 days. A = ?  
A) 25  B) 28  C) 30  D) 32  
**Answer: C** (1/A = 1/12 − 1/20 = 2/60 → A = 30)

**Q9.** Wages ₹7500. A:B work ratio = 3:2. A gets?  
A) ₹3000  B) ₹3500  C) ₹4500  D) ₹5000  
**Answer: C**

**Q10.** A does 2× work/day as B. Together: 8 days. A alone?  
A) 10  B) 12  C) 14  D) 16  
**Answer: B**

## 7. Exam Strategy

- Always convert to **per-day work rate** (1/n)
- Use **LCM method** for clarity
- Pipe problems: inlet (+), outlet (−)
- NTPC 2021 had toughest T&W — practice multi-step variants
