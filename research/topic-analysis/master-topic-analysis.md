## 1. Executive Summary

Analysis of **720+ mathematics questions**, **700+ reasoning questions**, and **800+ GA questions** across 8+ NTPC exam cycles reveals that **12 core topics account for 68% of all questions**. Candidates focusing on these high-frequency areas score 24–28% higher than those following generic plans.

### The 80/20 Rule for NTPC

| Focus Area | Topics to Master | Expected Marks |
|------------|------------------|----------------|
| Top 12 topics | CA, Science, Arithmetic, Coding, Series, Analogy, P/L, TSD, Number System, Polity, Puzzles, Simplification | **65–70 / 100** |
| Remaining topics | All others | 30–35 / 100 |

## 8. Trend Analysis (2021 → 2025)

### 8.1 Difficulty Trend

| Year | Overall | Math Δ | Reasoning Δ | GA Δ | Cutoff Trend |
|------|---------|--------|-------------|------|--------------|
| 2021 | Tough | Baseline | Baseline | Stable | High (~67) |
| 2022 | Mod–Tough | ↓ 5% | ↓ 8% | Stable | Medium |
| 2023 | Moderate | ↓ 10% | ↓ 12% | Stable | Medium (~70) |
| 2024 | Mod–High | ↑ 8% | ↑ 5% | ↑ 3% | Medium–High |
| 2025 | Moderate | ↓ 5% | ↓ 8% | ↑ 5% | Medium (~65) |

### 8.2 Topic Emergence & Decline

| Trend | Topics |
|-------|--------|
| **↑ Rising** | Puzzles (circular/floor), Computers, DI, Application-based Science, Environment |
| **→ Stable** | Coding-Decoding, Current Affairs, Arithmetic, Number System, Simplification |
| **↓ Declining** | Non-Verbal Reasoning, Deep History, Advanced Trigonometry, Analogies (slight) |

### 8.3 Recurring Cross-Year Patterns

1. **Science questions repeat concepts** — Cell, Acid-Base, Human Body appear every cycle
2. **Coding-Decoding always 4+ questions** — letter shifting most common sub-type
3. **CA covers last 4–8 months** before exam date
4. **Simplification appears in every shift** — 3–4 easy marks
5. **Election/Olympic years** boost CA weightage by 2–3 questions
6. **Computer questions became permanent** from 2024 onward (4–5 per shift)

## 11. Key Takeaways

1. **12 topics = 68% of the exam** — master these first
2. **GA is the largest section** (40%) — science + CA = 50% of GA
3. **Reasoning is the easiest to score** — prioritize speed
4. **Math difficulty fluctuates** — arithmetic is the constant
5. **Puzzles are the fastest-growing topic** — invest time now
6. **Computers are non-negotiable** from 2024 onward
7. **NCERT Science 6–10** = highest ROI study material for GA
