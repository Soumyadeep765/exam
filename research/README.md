## Quick Reference: NTPC CBT 1 Pattern

| Section | Questions | Marks | Time |
|---------|-----------|-------|------|
| General Awareness | 40 | 40 | 90 min total |
| Mathematics | 30 | 30 | |
| Reasoning | 30 | 30 | |
| **Total** | **100** | **100** | **90 min** |

- Negative marking: **1/3 per wrong answer**
- Languages: 15 (including Hindi, English)
- 21 RRB zones conduct independently

## Top 12 Topics (68% of Exam)

1. Current Affairs (GA)
2. General Science (GA)
3. Arithmetic / Simplification (Math)
4. Coding-Decoding (Reasoning)
5. Number System (Math)
6. Series & Analogy (Reasoning)
7. Profit & Loss (Math)
8. Puzzles (Reasoning)
9. Percentage / Ratio (Math)
10. Computers (GA)
11. Indian Polity (GA)
12. Time, Speed & Distance (Math)
