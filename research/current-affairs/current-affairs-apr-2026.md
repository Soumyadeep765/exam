## Topic 2: New Criminal Laws (BNS, BNSS, BSA)

### Summary
Three new criminal laws replaced colonial-era codes effective from **1 July 2024** (continued implementation and awareness in 2026). High-probability NTPC polity topic.

### Important Facts
- **BNS** (Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita) replaces **IPC** (Indian Penal Code)
- **BNSS** (Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita) replaces **CrPC** (Criminal Procedure Code)
- **BSA** (Bharatiya Sakshya Adhiniyam) replaces **Indian Evidence Act**
- Enacted December 2023; effective July 2024
- Sedition removed; new offenses for terrorism, organized crime
- Timelines for justice delivery mandated

### Possible NTPC Questions

**Q2. BNS replaces which colonial-era law?**
- A) CrPC
- B) IPC
- C) Indian Evidence Act
- D) Contract Act

**Answer: B**

**Q3. BNSS replaces:**
- A) IPC
- B) CrPC
- C) Evidence Act
- D) CPC

**Answer: B**

**Q4. BSA replaces:**
- A) Indian Penal Code
- B) Criminal Procedure Code
- C) Indian Evidence Act
- D) Constitution

**Answer: C**
