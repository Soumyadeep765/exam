## Topic 1: Republic Day 2026 (77th)

### Summary
India celebrated its **77th Republic Day** on 26 January 2026. **Ursula von der Leyen** (President, European Commission) and **António Costa** (President, European Council) were the chief guests — marking the first time EU leaders jointly attended as RD guests.

### Important Facts
- Republic Day commemorates adoption of the Constitution (26 January 1950)
- 2026 theme focused on **India-EU strategic partnership**
- Parade showcased indigenous defence systems and cultural diversity
- Beating Retreat ceremony on 29 January at Vijay Chowk

### Possible NTPC Questions

**Q1. Who were the chief guests at India's 77th Republic Day 2026?**
- A) US President and UK PM
- B) European Commission President and European Council President
- C) UN Secretary General and WHO Director
- D) ASEAN Secretary General and African Union Chair

**Answer: B**

**Q2. On which date did India adopt its Constitution?**
- A) 15 August 1947
- B) 26 January 1950
- C) 26 November 1949
- D) 2 October 1950

**Answer: B** (Constitution adopted 26 Nov 1949; came into force 26 Jan 1950)

## Topic 2: India-EU Free Trade Agreement

### Summary
On **26–27 January 2026**, India and the European Union finalized a landmark **Free Trade Agreement (FTA)** during an EU-India summit in Delhi. Ursula von der Leyen called it the **"mother of all deals."**

### Important Facts
- Covers **2 billion people** — largest FTA zone by population
- Tariff reduction on **96.6% of goods** traded by value
- Key sectors: iron & steel, plastics, chemicals, machinery, pharmaceuticals, cars, wine
- EU exports to India expected to **double by 2032**
- EU companies save ~**€4 billion** annually in duties
- Includes **mobility agreement** for Indian students and professionals across 27 EU states
- Tech cooperation: semiconductors, AI, quantum, 6G, clean tech, cybersecurity

### Possible NTPC Questions

**Q3. India finalized a major FTA with which bloc in January 2026?**
- A) ASEAN
- B) European Union
- C) African Union
- D) MERCOSUR

**Answer: B**

**Q4. Approximately what percentage of goods traded between India and EU will see tariff reduction under the 2026 FTA?**
- A) 75%
- B) 85%
- C) 96.6%
- D) 100%

**Answer: C**

## Topic 3: President's Address to Parliament (28 January 2026)

### Summary
President **Droupadi Murmu** addressed the joint sitting of Parliament on 28 January 2026, outlining government achievements and policy objectives for 2026–27.

### Important Facts
- **SHANTI Act** enacted — target of **100 GW nuclear energy by 2047**
- **PLI scheme:** ₹2 lakh crore investment attracted; ₹17 lakh crore production achieved
- Electronics production reached **₹11 lakh crore** (6× growth in 11 years)
- India became **2nd largest mobile manufacturer**
- **4 new semiconductor units** sanctioned in 2025 (10 total planned)
- **PM Surya Ghar:** ~20 lakh rooftop solar systems installed
- **Ujjwala:** 10+ crore LPG connections
- Defence production crossed **₹1.5 lakh crore**; exports **₹23,000 crore**
- Maoist-affected districts reduced from **126 to 8**
- **Jan Vishwas Act 2025:** 300+ offenses decriminalized
- GST: Income above **₹12 lakh** exempted from tax (announced)

### Possible NTPC Questions

**Q5. The SHANTI Act aims to achieve how much nuclear energy capacity by 2047?**
- A) 50 GW
- B) 75 GW
- C) 100 GW
- D) 150 GW

**Answer: C**

**Q6. Under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, approximately how many rooftop solar systems have been installed?**
- A) 5 lakh
- B) 10 lakh
- C) 20 lakh
- D) 50 lakh

**Answer: C**
