## Topic 1: World Environment Day (5 June)

### Summary
**5 June 2026** — World Environment Day (UN). Theme focused on land restoration, desertification, and drought resilience. India highlighted green energy and conservation efforts.

### Important Facts
- UN World Environment Day: 5 June (since 1973)
- 2024 host was Saudi Arabia; themes rotate globally
- India focus: green hydrogen, solar, afforestation
- **LiFE Mission:** Lifestyle for Environment (PM Modi)
- **National Green Hydrogen Mission:** 5 MMT target by 2030
- NTPC: green ammonia, renewable capacity expansion

### Possible NTPC Questions

**Q1. World Environment Day is observed on:**
- A) 5 June
- B) 22 April
- C) 2 October
- D) 11 December

**Answer: A**

**Q2. Which company achieved India's first large-scale green ammonia supply deal under SIGHT scheme?**
- A) ONGC
- B) NTPC
- C) BHEL
- D) Coal India

**Answer: B**

## Topic 3: Monsoon & Agriculture Update

### Summary
**June** marks the arrival of **Southwest Monsoon** in India. IMD forecasts, kharif sowing, and crop insurance schemes in focus.

### Important Facts
- Monsoon arrival: typically **1 June** (Kerala coast)
- IMD: India Meteorological Department
- PMFBY: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (crop insurance)
- Kharif crops: rice, maize, cotton, soybean, groundnut
- Monsoon contributes ~70% of India's annual rainfall

### Possible NTPC Questions

**Q6. Southwest Monsoon typically arrives first in:**
- A) Mumbai
- B) Chennai
- C) Kerala
- D) Kolkata

**Answer: C**

**Q7. PMFBY is a scheme for:**
- A) Crop insurance
- B) Crop procurement
- C) Crop export
- D) Crop research

**Answer: A**

## Topic 4: International Yoga Day (21 June)

### Summary
**21 June** — International Day of Yoga (UN). India's soft power and wellness diplomacy highlighted.

### Important Facts
- UN declared 21 June as International Yoga Day in **2014**
- PM Modi proposed at UN General Assembly (2014)
- First celebrated: **2015**
- Yoga originated in ancient India
- Ministry of AYUSH coordinates events

## Topic 5: RRB NTPC Exam Season

### Summary
June is historically a major month for **RRB NTPC CBT examinations**. 2025 CBT 1 ran 5–24 June. Aspirants should track official RRB websites for 2026 cycle updates.

### Important Facts
- RRB websites: 21 regional boards (rrbcdg.gov.in, etc.)
- Exam: 100 MCQ, 90 min, CBT mode
- 15 languages available
- Normalization across shifts
- Admit card: 4 days before exam date
- Results: region-wise, on official RRB sites
