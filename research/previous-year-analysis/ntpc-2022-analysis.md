|-------|-----------|------------|-------|

### Difficulty Profile
- Slightly **easier than 2021 CBT 1** but more questions
- DI appeared more consistently (2–3 questions)
- Mensuration had formula-based direct questions

### Repeated Question Patterns
1. CI vs SI difference for 2 years
2. Ratio in mixture/alligation
3. Time & Work with wages distribution
4. Mensuration: cone + cylinder composite figures
5. DI table interpretation with percentage calculation

## 8. Preparation Takeaways

1. **CBT 2 requires speed** — 45 seconds per question average
2. **GA becomes even more critical** at 50 questions
3. **DI preparation is mandatory** for CBT 2 aspirants
4. **Puzzle practice** should include linear, circular, and floor-based
5. **2022 confirmed the trend** — science + CA dominate GA section
