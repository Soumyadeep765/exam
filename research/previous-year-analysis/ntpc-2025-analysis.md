## 1. Exam Overview

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Total Questions | 100 |
| Duration | 90 minutes |
| Applicants | 1.2+ crore |
| Sections | GA (40) + Math (30) + Reasoning (30) |
| Overall Difficulty | **Moderate** (most shifts) |
| Negative Marking | 1/3 per wrong answer |
| CA Window | October 2024 – April 2025 (primary) |

**Key Insight:** 2025 CBT 1 was **moderate and balanced**. Reasoning was the easiest section across most shifts. GA was CA-heavy with significant computer and science blocks. Mathematics was formula-based and solvable with practice.

## 9. Preparation Takeaways

1. **CA window: 4–6 months before exam** — October to April for June exams
2. **Circular puzzles are the #1 reasoning priority** for upcoming cycles
3. **Computer questions are now 4–5 fixed** — create a cheat sheet
4. **Simplification = free marks** — 4–5 easy questions every shift
5. **Science + CA overlap** — prepare science current events (missions, discoveries)
6. **72–82 good attempts** target for General category in moderate shifts
